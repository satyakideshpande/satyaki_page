About Me

Satyaki Deshpande

Hello everyone ! My name is Satyaki Deshpande and I am from Pune Maharashtra.

I have been writing for about 3-4 years. Writing has given me a vision to see things far beyond the human eye. I usually write,blogs, short-stories,poems and shayari's and post them on Instagram so that my readers can read them. I am honored by the response I get from people and hope to write more content that people can relate too!

Being a youth and belonging to the computer science community. I have a deep desire to learn and implement what I've learned and hopefully one day make a name for myself in this feild.

Other than having a strong thirst in the computer feild, I am a sports enthusiast and have played almost all kinds of sports.

Journey

Till now .

  • 2014

    My Humble Beginnings

    At the start of my writing journey, I used to write small motivational quotes on facebook. This was the time when facebook was highly popular. I had no idea I would be where I am today !

  • April 2015

    Change is inevitable

    After about a year of writing small motivational quotes, it was time to do something different. So, I started writing blogs on Facebook on various social issues.

  • October 2017

    A fire was lit !

    People had started to notice me and it was time to take the game to the next level. So I joined Instagram where I started writing short stories. The response I got from my auidence was incredible !

  • 2017 till now

    A 'kingdom' was made !

    By this time I had not only started writing short-stories but had also started shayaris,poems and write-up's. People started admiring my work and could relate to the content as if it was written based on them.

    I am absolutely blessed by the love and respect I recieve from my readers. They give me the power to write more.
